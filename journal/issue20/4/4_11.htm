4.11 Mandres: Hellenistic to Roman

4.11 Mandres: Hellenistic to Roman

Activity during the Hellenistic to Roman periods is concentrated in the region of Kato Koutraphas Mandres (TS07). This is a relatively thin scatter, but extends quite widely. It may be evidence for cultivation, manuring or other extensive activities across the area.
