Online Data

A large portion of the data from the Troodos Archaeological and Environmental Survey Project has been provided online via this article as well as being offered for download via the related Digital Archive.

The online data includes geomorphological data, descriptions of POSIs and buildings, and much more. The pottery catalogue, for example, includes descriptions of 712 significant or representative sherds, and includes 663 artefact drawings. The commentaries in the guided tour provide links to particularly relevant online data records but these individual interfaces also allow you to do your own searches of the data and create your own queries.
