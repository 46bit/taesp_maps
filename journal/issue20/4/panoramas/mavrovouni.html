Panorama of Mavrovouni (TS02), taken from the Rock of Ayios Kyriakos (TP017), 25 March 2006.
The panorama begins looking north down the Lagoudhera valley to the village of Vyzakia. The black slag heap (TP006) is visible just to the right of the hidden bend in the track. To the right is a pit filled with water discoloured by sulphates, and then the spoil heap of the 20th-century mine of Memi. Further to the right the valley slopes are intensely terraced, some stone-built and some bulldozed, and are planted mainly with olives and almonds. Photographs: Michael Given. Panorama creation: Hugh Corley.
