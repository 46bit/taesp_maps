Panorama of Mandres area (TS07), 25 March 2006.
In the immediate foreground all round are the threshing floors (TP122) on the southern edge of the settlement. The panorama starts with the core of the settlement (TP021) and the view north-west towards Morphou Bay. Scrolling right past the tractors you see one of the outlier houses (BU0006), and then more threshing floors, one of them (with the car) shaded by a eucalyptus tree. Another outlying house (BU0007) lies beyond it. Back in the core of the settlement, one of the houses has a new superstructure built for military exercises. Photographs: Michael Given. Panorama creation: Hugh Corley.
