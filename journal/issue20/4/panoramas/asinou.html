Panorama of Asinou Valley, 25 March 2006.
From Asinou village (TS03; the white building has been rebuilt) scroll rightwards, where some of the houses of Pera Yitonia (TP061) on the other side of the valley are visible. Further right some white forestry cairns mark the boundary between the state forest and the village territory. Just past the valley's exit onto the plains to the north is the church of Panayia Phorviotissa (TP030), with tour bus and restaurant. Photographs: Michael Given. Panorama creation: Hugh Corley.
