Retaining Features

Earth-retention features abound in the Cypriot landscape, and many of these are difficult to recognize as cultural in origin. Through geomorphic inspection of the lower terraces along streams and rivers we have found that many are cultural in origin and held in place or were held in place, by walls of various constructions. Hence, their documentation is critical to the geomorphological analysis of the landscape. The categories listed here include all of the recognized (to date) types of structures.
