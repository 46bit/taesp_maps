Background

An estimation of background confusion on ground in unit. An indication of how often a field worker bends to pick up a sherd only to find that it is a leaf, or a stone, or anything but pottery.

CodeValue
0Never
1Occasionally
2Frequently
3All the time. So much that you give up bending down.
